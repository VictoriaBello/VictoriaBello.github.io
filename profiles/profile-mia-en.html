Biography

Mia Skogster is one of the most influential and respected IGP competitors and trainers worldwide. Originally from Finland, she has dedicated her life to dog sport, focusing on emotional communication and creating a state of "flow" with the dog. Her method, which combines high technical precision with exceptional motivation, has taken her to the top of the sport. Her dogs are known for energetic, harmonious, and perfectly controlled obedience routines, earning multiple "Best Obedience" awards in world championships.
