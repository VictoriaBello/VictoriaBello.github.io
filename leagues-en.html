Main IGP Organizations

Discover the organizations that regulate and organize IGP competitions worldwide.

FCI (Fédération Cynologique Internationale)

The largest canine organization in the world. Regulates all kinds of canine sports and organizes its own World Championship.

Official FCI website
FCA (Argentine Cynological Federation)

The national entity representing the FCI in Argentina. Organizes local events under its regulations and approves titles and competitions in the country.

Official FCA website
WUSV (World Union of German Shepherd Dog Clubs)

Exclusive organization for the German Shepherd breed. WUSV organizes its own IGP World Championship, an important event for breed enthusiasts.

Official WUSV website
POA (German Shepherd Club Argentina)

Main breed club that organizes IGP competitions under FCA and FCI regulations in Argentina.

Official POA website
FMBB (Fédération Mondiale du Berger Belge)

Global organization for Belgian Shepherd breeds. Hosts its own IGP World Championship, the main event for Malinois Belgian Shepherds.

Official FMBB website
COB (Belgian Shepherd Club Argentina)

Main breed club organizing IGP competitions under FCA and FCI regulations in Argentina.

Official COB website
RSV2000

German organization focused on training science and genetic health of working dogs.

Official RSV Global website
RSV Global Latin America

RSV2000 representation in the region, responsible for organizing events and seminars under the same regulations in Latin America, including Argentina.

Official RSV Global Latin America website
