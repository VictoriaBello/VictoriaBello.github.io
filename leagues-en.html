Main IGP Organizations

Learn about the organizations that regulate and organize IGP competitions worldwide.

FCI (Fédération Cynologique Internationale)

The world's largest canine organization. It regulates all types of dog sports and organizes its own World Championship.

Official FCI Website
FCA (Federación Cinológica Argentina)

The national entity representing the FCI in Argentina. It organizes local events under its regulations and endorses titles and competitions in the country.

Official FCA Website
WUSV (World Union of German Shepherd Dog Clubs)

Exclusive organization for the German Shepherd breed. WUSV organizes its own IGP World Championship, considered a major event for breed enthusiasts.

Official WUSV Website
POA (German Shepherd Club of Argentina)

Main breed club organizing IGP competitions under FCA and FCI regulations in Argentina.

Official POA Website
FMBB (Fédération Mondiale du Berger Belge)

Global organization for Belgian Shepherd breeds. It hosts its own IGP World Championship, the most important event for Malinois enthusiasts.

Official FMBB Website
COB (Belgian Shepherd Club)

Main breed club organizing IGP competitions under FCA and FCI regulations in Argentina.

Official COB Website
RSV2000

German organization focused on training science and genetic health of working dogs.

Official RSV Global Website
RSV Global Latin America

RSV2000 representation in the region, organizing events and seminars under the same regulations in Latin America, including Argentina.

Official RSV Global Latin America Website
