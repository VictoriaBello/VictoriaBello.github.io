Main IGP Organizations

IGP is practiced under different rules and organizations worldwide. Knowing them is key to understanding competitions and standards for breeding and training.

FCI (Fédération Cynologique Internationale)

The largest canine organization in the world. It regulates all types of dog sports and organizes its own World Championship.

  • Official FCI website

FCA (Federación Cinológica Argentina)

The national entity representing FCI in Argentina. It organizes local events under its regulations and endorses titles and competitions in the country.

  • Official FCA website

POA (German Shepherd Club)

Main breed club organizing IGP competitions under FCA and FCI regulations in Argentina.

  • Official POA website

COB (Belgian Shepherd Club)

Main breed club organizing IGP competitions under FCA and FCI regulations in Argentina.

  • Official COB website

WUSV (World Union of German Shepherd Clubs)

Exclusive organization for the German Shepherd breed. WUSV organizes its own IGP World Championship, highly important for enthusiasts of the breed.

  • Official WUSV website

FMBB (Fédération Mondiale du Berger Belge)

Global organization for Belgian Shepherd breeds. Hosts its own IGP World Championship, the most important event for Malinois in the sport.

  • Official FMBB website

RSV2000

German organization focused on training science and the genetic health of working dogs.

  • Official RSV Global website

RSV Global Latin America

RSV2000's representation in the region, responsible for organizing events and seminars under the same rules in Latin America, including Argentina.

  • Official RSV Global Latin America website
